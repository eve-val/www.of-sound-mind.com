SOUND JF Service: KNEES

SOUND operates a corp-owned Jump Freight service, named Never Bend The Deadline, with ticker KNEES. This JF service generally runs from the nearest edge of highsec to our staging points in lowsec or nullsec. We generally don't provide service direct to/from trade hubs; for that, use a public courier or Red Frog/PushX to move your cargo to the highsec jumpoff point first. This is less convenient for you, but more convenient and safer for our jump freight pilots.

Terms of Service

KNEES couriers are always best-effort, and delivery (especially timely delivery) is subject to JF pilots feeling it is safe to do so. Only senior SOUND members are allowed to become KNEES members, and as such KNEES generally runs without collateral on contracts, with the corp itself insuring them against loss in any way (theft, JF gank, etc). Collateral is allowed, especially for non-SOUND members, but setting high collaterals may delay your courier.

Unless you arrange otherwise in advance with a specific JF pilot, all contracts must be:

  • Availability: private to Never Bend the Deadline [KNEES] (double-check this)
  • Volume: <= 330,000m3
  • Ship To: one of the endpoints listed below
  • Ship From: one of the endpoints listed below
  • Reward: >= the ISK/m3 costs given below + 1% of collateral (if set)
  • Expiration: 1 week or more
  • Days to complete: 3 or more

Syndicate Routes

These routes connect highsec with various staging points in Syndicate. Specifically, KNEES runs contracts in all directions between any of:

  • Adacyne III - Moon 14 (NPC Station, Top)
  • Chardalane V (NPC Station, Bottom)
  • 0LTQ-C - Hayabusa (SOUND Fortizar)
  • 1-3HWZ - Sojourner (BIGAB Astra)
  • 2Q-I6Q (BIGAB Fortizar)
  • LSC4-P I (NPC Station)
  • LSC4-P - International Space Station (SOUND Tatara)
  • X-M2LR (BIGAB Fortizar)

The price for routes starting in Adacyne is 100 ISK/m3, and all other routes are currently free.

Deployment Routes

KNEES does not currently service any deployment routes.
